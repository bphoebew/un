Projects Under Goal 2

Los Jorneleros

Our project aims to provide lunches for the day laborers so their food does not need to be included in their pay. We must show that we care about that community in a city that has repeatedly attacked them. (Founded by Dane Legassick)

Comida de la Calle

Due to laws against street food vendors, students are taking the initiative to support their micro-economy. (Founded by Jake Gallardo and Brandon Isles)

Food for Thought

We aim to arrange lunches and dinners at our local homeless shelter, Bridge to Home. (Founded by Kait Wilson)
