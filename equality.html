Catcalls of SCV

We will create an Instagram account called “@catcallsofscv” in which we accept direct messages from any teenager who has experienced catcalling in Santa Clarita. We will ask for what was said, the location at which it was said, and whether or not the person wishes to be anonymous. We will take a weekend to drive around and write the quote in chalk in the location it was said. We will post pictures of the chalk along with a screenshot of the message, and occasionally videos of the chalking. Lastly, we made a website linking to our Instagram that also includes a documentary about the experience. (Quynn Lubs)

S(HE)TEM

The fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics are primarily dominated by males. We hope to change this by making STEM seem less exclusive and intimidating to girls by giving them access to numerous resources and encouraging them to help bridge the gender gap in STEM. (Bridget and Phoebe)
