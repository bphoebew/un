The Oasis

Born and raised in affluent Santa Clarita, the contrast between low income families and the rest of the city has become increasingly apparent. The Oasis aims to bridge the gap between these two divisions of the community through a communal collection of clothing, essentials and food raised in support of lower income families and individuals of our local community. (Co-directed by Kait Wilson and Cassidy Bensko)

Trash For Change

Our mission is to create sustainable programs among our peers to implement waste management bins with routine helpers. (Founded by Evan Diaz)
