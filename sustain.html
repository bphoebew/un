Rethinking LA Traffic

LA traffic, and traffic in general, has multiple negative consequences including economical, psychological, and environmental effects on the community as a whole. These include pollution, road rage, and mental health issues. Most of these problems can be solved due to the small area that this technology would be implemented in. Solutions include an adjustable height of the bus, traffic light timing, and the development of an entirely new traffic light. (JJ Oh, Derrick Borden, Mason Moon)
