Pasta Not VAPE

Teenagers first begin to vape for one reason: it looks cool. Young people are so easily influenced to simply fit in in the beginning. By replacing these vape pens with different variations of pasta, we’re simultaneously creating a new fad and mocking those who quit what they prefer and make decisions just because it’s “trending” or the “norm.” (Tammy Chung)
